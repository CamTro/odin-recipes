Hunters Chicken

Description

Hunters Chicken is a British dish made of chicken breasts wrapped in bacon, with barbecue sauce and melted cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Streaky Bacon
  • Grated Cheese
  • Barbecue Sauce

Alternatively, a homemade sauce can be made with the following ingredients:

  • Tomato ketchup
  • Honey
  • Mustard powder
  • Dark brown sugar
  • Apple cider or white wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and wrap each chicken breast with bacon.
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients together (if you are making a homemade sauce).
  3. Place your chicken breasts in the oven for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Cook the sauce on the hob while the chicken is in the oven.
  5. After time has passed, take chicken breasts out of the oven, pour the sauce on top and add melted cheese before returning to oven for cheese to melt.
